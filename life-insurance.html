Family Protection First

Life Insurance Plans for Every Stage of Life

Life insurance is more than just a policy — it's a promise to your loved ones. At BNI Services, we believe every family deserves the right life cover that ensures financial security when it matters most, without overpaying for unnecessary coverage.

Our advisors take time to understand your family structure, liabilities, income, and long-term goals before recommending any plan. We work with top insurers like LIC, HDFC Life, ICICI Prudential, and more to give you the best options.

Life Insurance Plans We Offer

Term Insurance

Pure life cover at the lowest premium — high sum assured for complete family protection.

ULIP Plans

Unit Linked Insurance Plans combining investment and life cover in one product.

Endowment Plans

Savings + life cover — get a lump sum on maturity or family protection on unfortunate demise.

Retirement Plans

Build a guaranteed pension corpus for a stress-free, financially independent retirement.

Child Plans

Secure your child's education and future milestones with dedicated child insurance plans.

Whole Life Plans

Lifetime coverage with maturity benefit — protection for the entire life of the insured.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Mutual Fund Advisory

Mutual funds are one of the most powerful tools for long-term wealth creation. At BNI Services, we analyse your financial goals, risk appetite, and investment horizon to create a personalized portfolio that works hard for your money — consistently.

Whether you're just starting your SIP journey or looking to rebalance an existing portfolio, our AMFI-registered advisors provide end-to-end guidance backed by market research and data-driven insights.

Types of Funds We Offer

Equity Funds

High growth potential for long-term investors with moderate to high risk tolerance.

Debt Funds

Stable returns with lower risk — ideal for short-to-medium term financial goals.

Hybrid Funds

Balanced mix of equity and debt for diversified, steady growth portfolios.

ELSS Funds

Tax-saving mutual funds under Section 80C with potential for equity-level returns.

Index Funds

Low-cost market tracking funds mirroring indices like Nifty 50 and Sensex.

Liquid Funds

Park your surplus cash with better returns than savings accounts — high liquidity.

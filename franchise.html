FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need prior experience in finance?
No prior finance experience is required. We provide full training — from product knowledge to sales techniques. A willingness to learn and connect with people is all you need.
What is the minimum investment to start?
Our Starter Plan begins at ₹25,000. This covers the franchise fee, registration support, and basic training. Higher plans offer more tools, leads, and earning potential.
Can I run this as a part-time business?
Yes. Many of our franchise partners started part-time and scaled to full-time once their earnings exceeded their primary income. The model is flexible.
Will BNI Services share leads with me?
Growth and Premium plan partners receive regional leads shared by the BNI head office. Starter plan partners can upgrade to access lead sharing after 3 months.
Is there a renewal fee every year?
There is a nominal annual renewal fee which is waived for partners who meet their annual target. Details are covered in the franchise agreement.

Terms & Conditions

Franchise Agreement Terms

Please read the following terms carefully before applying for the BNI Services franchise.

Office Space & Operations
The franchise partner is responsible for arranging and maintaining a minimum 200 sq. ft. office space. All local registrations, licenses, office rent, utilities, internet, operational expenses, and staffing (if required) shall be the sole responsibility of the franchise partner.
Company Responsibilities
The company will provide complete software, technology, business setup assistance, professional training, marketing support, and ongoing technical support to enable the franchise partner to operate the business effectively.
Ownership of Assets
The company shall retain sole ownership of all software, applications, equipment, databases, and other assets provided under this business package. The franchise partner shall use all company-provided resources only for authorized business purposes as specified in the agreement.
Franchise Fee & Security Deposit
  1. The Franchise Package Fee of ₹99,000 is strictly non-refundable under any circumstances.
  2. The ₹50,000 Security Deposit is refundable only after successful completion or termination of the agreement, subject to the return of all company assets and fulfillment of all contractual obligations.
  3. No interest shall be payable on the security deposit or on any outstanding amount.
Service Modifications
The company reserves the right to modify or discontinue any service, software, or business process whenever necessary to improve operations. Partners will be notified of significant changes in advance wherever possible.
Income & Earnings
Earnings are performance-based and depend on sales, business efforts, location, and market conditions. Actual income may vary. The figure of "Earn up to ₹50,000 in your first month" is an estimated earning potential based on business performance — it is not a guaranteed income.

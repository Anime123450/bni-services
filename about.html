Who We Are

Your Partner in Financial Well-being

BNI Insurance & Investment Services was founded in 2013 with a clear mission: to make professional financial advisory accessible, transparent, and impactful for every individual and business in Vadodara and beyond.

What started as a small advisory practice has grown into a trusted financial services firm with a team of certified professionals, serving 500+ happy clients across mutual fund investments, life insurance, general insurance, and business consulting.

We are AMFI-registered for mutual fund advisory and IRDAI-authorised for insurance services — ensuring every recommendation we make is backed by regulatory compliance, market expertise, and genuine client interest.

Our philosophy is simple: we succeed only when our clients succeed. That's why we focus on long-term relationships over transactional interactions, and why our clients trust us with their most important financial decisions.

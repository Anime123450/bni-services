FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries. Still have questions? Just call or WhatsApp us.

Is a consultation with BNI Services free?
Yes, absolutely! Our initial consultation is completely free with no obligation. We believe every individual deserves access to proper financial guidance, which is why we offer a no-cost, no-commitment advisory session.
What is the minimum amount to start a SIP?
You can start a Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) with as little as ₹100 to ₹500 per month, depending on the mutual fund. We help you choose the right fund and amount based on your income and financial goals.
How do you help with insurance claims?
We provide end-to-end claim support — from intimating the insurer and gathering documents to following up until the claim is settled. We represent you and ensure you receive the full benefit you're entitled to. Our 98% claim success rate speaks for itself.
Are you AMFI registered and IRDAI authorised?
Yes! BNI Services is AMFI (Association of Mutual Funds in India) registered for mutual fund advisory and IRDAI (Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India) authorised for selling and servicing insurance policies. All our advisors hold valid certifications.
Can I switch my existing investments to BNI Services?
Absolutely. We can review your existing mutual fund portfolio, insurance policies, and financial plan — and help you optimize or transfer them. Portfolio transfers are generally seamless and often come with no additional cost.
